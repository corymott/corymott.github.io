Meet Chef Santi!

Chef Santi is a world trained chef, working in Paris, Rome, and Chicago. He is using his garnered experience to bring world class cuisine to Sbarra Italiano.


Chef Santi's signature dish is his famous Wood-Fired Pizza, with his mother's lasagna and fathers tortellini coming in a close second and third. These dishes are sure to make your day when you come to eat at Sbarra Italiano.
